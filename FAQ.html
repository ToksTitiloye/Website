Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about how my coaching works.

Who are these sessions for?

These sessions are for players aiming to improve in League of Legends (up to Master Jungle and Diamond in other roles) and VALORANT (all roles, up to Immortal 3).

Does my region matter?

No. I can coach players from any region. Book a time that suits you, and it’ll be automatically translated to my timezone.

What’s your refund/cancellation policy?

If something comes up and you can’t attend, please cancel 24 hours in advance. I’ll happily reschedule or refund.

What games do you coach?

I currently offer coaching for League of Legends and VALORANT.

Why are you qualified to coach people?

I’ve reached Master tier in League of Legends and consistently maintained Immortal 3 in VALORANT. Over the years, I’ve coached more than 100 players across both games, helping them climb and improve with structured feedback and tailored training. I’ve also worked with new VALORANT teams, guiding them through fundamentals of teamplay, communication, and strategy. My mix of high-rank experience and hands-on coaching gives me the knowledge to help players at every stage of their journey.
